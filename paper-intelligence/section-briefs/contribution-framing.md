# Contribution Framing Brief

## Strong Current Contribution Candidates

- Runtime optimizer for compound AI systems, operating on multi-call traces rather than isolated prompts.
- Runtime interception and optimization of LLM agent calls with minimal application changes.
- Conservative planner that proposes rewrites only after repeated observations and rule-specific checks.
- Empirical rule-level savings for ContextCompress and ModelDowngrade.
- Boundary evidence showing rules do not need to fire when expected savings are low.

## Not Ready Yet

- Broad claims that AgentC optimizes all agent workloads.
- Claims that all rules have equivalent empirical support.
- Strong novelty claims without related-work verification.
- "First runtime optimizer" unless carefully narrowed against Autellix/Halo and rule-specific prior art.
